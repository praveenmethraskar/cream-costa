About CREAM COSTA

Crafted for Cream Costa - Classic, Fresh & Joyful

At Cream Costa, ice cream is more than just a dessert — it's a feeling, a celebration, and a moment worth remembering. What began as a small dream in Bommasandra, Bengaluru has grown into a brand cherished for its authentic flavours, premium quality, and heart-warming experience.

With a passion for crafting happiness in every scoop, we create ice creams using finest ingredients, real fruits, traditional Indian flavours, and rich creamy bases. Our catalogue includes beloved favorites like Vanilla, Butterscotch, Chocolate, and exciting innovations like Rajbhog, Salted Caramel, Tender Coconut, and more — all prepared to deliver freshness and joy in every bite, as reflected in our 4L, family, cup, and stick ranges.
